## Overview
This project showcases an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built in Power BI, designed to provide insights into sales trends, profitability, and customer behavior across different segments and locations.

## Key Features

### KPI Cards
- **Total Sales**
- **Total Profit**
- **Profit Margin %** - profit as a percentage of sales  
- **Sales Growth %** - growth compared to previous period  
- **Avg Order Value**
- **Total Cost**

### Visualizations
- **Sales Trend (Current vs Last Year)** - comparison of sales over time  
- **Sales by City** – sales distribution by city with growth indicators  
- **Map Visualization**
- **Profit by Customer Segment (Decomposition Tree)** – breakdown of profit by customer segments  
- **Top 5 Products by Sales** - best-selling products

### Filters (Slicers)
- **Year**  
- **Customer Segment**  
- **City**

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI**
- **Excel**
- **DAX**
- **Data Cleaning & Transformation**
- **Data Modeling**
- **Data Visualization**

## Key Insights
- **Strong sales growth in selected cities**
- **Corporate segment generates the highest profit**
- **Clear seasonal trends in sales performance**
